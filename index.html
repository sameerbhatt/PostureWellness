Your Data Never Leaves Your Device

Posture Wellness is built with privacy as a core principle. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data to the cloud.

All posture analysis happens locally using Apple's Vision framework. Your camera captures are processed immediately and discarded — never saved unless you explicitly choose to review them.

First time opening on macOS?

Since this app is independently distributed, macOS will show a security warning.

  1. After downloading, open the app
  2. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security
  3. Scroll down and click "Open Anyway"
  4. Enter your Mac password when prompted

This is a one-time step. The app runs fully locally. No data ever leaves your Mac. If there is enough interest, I will submit the app to the Mac App Store for easier installation and automatic updates.
